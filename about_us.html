Our Story

Jaiho Digital was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize advanced technology and make it accessible to everyone. Founded in 2020 by a fresh computer science graduate, we started as a small initiative to bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and practical applications.

What began as a solo journey to explore AI and software development has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem of specialized companies. Each subsidiary represents a different facet of our technological ambitions, from AI product development to advanced research and development.

Our founder's journey from B.Tech graduate to building a multi-faceted technology company reflects our core belief: that innovation comes from persistence, curiosity, and the courage to dream big.

Today, we stand at the threshold of an exciting future, with plans that stretch from artificial general intelligence to space exploration. We're not just building products; we're crafting the tools that will shape humanity's next chapter.

Our Journey

Milestones that have shaped our growth and evolution into a comprehensive technology ecosystem

2020

Foundation of Jaiho Digital

Company founded by a B.Tech Computer Science graduate with a vision to democratize advanced technology. Started with core focus on AI and software development.

2021

Launch of Jaiho Labs

Established our product development division focusing on AI, machine learning, and innovative software solutions. First major step towards building a comprehensive technology ecosystem.

2022

Jaiho TechServe Expansion

Launched our service-oriented division to provide custom software development, web solutions, and technical consulting services to businesses worldwide.

2023

Jaiho Edge Development

Expanded into electronics and embedded systems, focusing on IoT solutions, robotics, and specialized hardware development including mini color lab machines.

2024

Jaiho Kernel R&D Initiative

Established our research and development arm specializing in reverse engineering, security testing, quality assurance, and open-source contributions.

2025

Jaiho Reach Marketing Launch

Launched our marketing and outreach division to help businesses establish complete digital presence. Now offering 100 days of free website development for small businesses.

Present

Ecosystem Maturation

All five Our Universe actively operating and growing. Research hub established, comprehensive services portfolio completed, and ambitious future roadmap in development.
