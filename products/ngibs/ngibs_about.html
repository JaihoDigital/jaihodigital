What is NGIBS?

NGIBS (Next Generation Intelligent Browsing System) is an advanced AI-powered search platform that combines multiple AI models with intelligent search capabilities. Built for researchers, developers, students, and knowledge workers, NGIBS offers a seamless experience for getting accurate, contextual answers to complex queries.

Mission: To democratize access to advanced AI capabilities and make intelligent search accessible to everyone, while maintaining transparency and user control over AI model selection.
Open Source: NGIBS is built with open-source principles, ensuring transparency, community contribution, and continuous improvement.
