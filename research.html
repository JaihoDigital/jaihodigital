Research & Development

Architecting
Intelligence.

From optimizing code execution to theorizing Artificial Super Intelligence (ASI), we operate at the bleeding edge of computer science. We don't just use APIs; we build the models, algorithms, and infrastructure that power the future.

Core Disciplines

Our R&D efforts are distributed across seven key pillars of innovation.

Generative AI & Multimodality

We are investigating new architectures for text-to-video and text-to-image synthesis. Our focus is on optimizing diffusion models (Fission AI) to run efficiently on consumer hardware.

Quantum Computing

Researching lattice-based cryptography to secure our Jaiho Kernel infrastructure against post-quantum decryption threats.

NLP & Linguistics

Bridging ancient philosophy with modern computation. Project Gita-py explores structured knowledge extraction from Sanskrit texts.

Robotics & IoT

Jaiho Edge: Developing embedded intelligence for autonomous systems and smart hardware that processes data locally.

Computer Vision

Real-time object detection and segmentation. Radiant AI pushes the limits of what browser-based vision models can achieve.

Software Engineering

Building the tools that build the tools. From Dev Link to custom compilers, we optimize the developer experience.

The Horizon: AGI & ASI

Our long-term theoretical research focuses on Artificial General Intelligence and Super Intelligence. We are exploring cognitive architectures, memory systems, and self-improving agents (SPA AI) to move beyond narrow tasks into generalized reasoning.
