Core Disciplines

Generative AI & Multimodality

We are investigating new architectures for text-to-video synthesis. Our product Fission AI serves as the testing ground for these models, integrating Stable Diffusion and BLIP for seamless creation.

Preparing for the post-quantum era. Researching lattice-based cryptography to secure our Jaiho Kernel infrastructure against future quantum threats.
