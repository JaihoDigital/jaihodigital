Future Roadmap

A Blueprint for
Tomorrow.

Our roadmap isn't just about software updates; it's about shifting the paradigm of how humans interact with digital and physical intelligence.

Pillar 1: Tech For Earth

Sustainability is the new standard.

The AI revolution consumes massive amounts of energy. At Jaiho Digital, we are committed to Green Computing.

  • ✅ Serverless First: Our "Kernel" tools run on-demand, reducing idle energy waste by 80%.
  • ✅ Optimized Models: "Labs" focuses on Small Language Models (SLMs) that perform efficiently on consumer hardware.

Pillar 2: Tech For All

Democratizing access to intelligence.

Technology is a human right. We break down barriers through:

  • ✅ Open Source: Releasing tools like Jaiho Pulse and Gita-py for free to the community.
  • ✅ Education: Platforms like ITARC and NLP Intelligent Lab teach the next generation of engineers.

The Roadmap

Phase 1: Foundation (2024-25)

Establishing the Core Subsidiaries (Labs, Kernel, TechServe). Launching key utilities like Pulse and IP.

Phase 2: Expansion (2026)

Launching Jaiho Edge hardware prototypes. Releasing enterprise-grade AI models via Fission.

Phase 3: Integration (2028+)

Seamless merging of physical (Edge) and digital (Labs) systems. Achieving net-zero carbon footprint.
